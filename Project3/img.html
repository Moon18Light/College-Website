About us

Welcome to Santosh College of Teacher’s Training & Education (SCTTE)

Santosh College of Teacher’s Training & Education (SCTTE) is being run by Society for Nature Education & Health (SNEH) and was registered in the year 2002 under society Registration Act XXI of 1860 (Reg. No II/1413). Santosh College of Teacher’s Training & Education being approved by NCTE and affiliated to Ranchi University, Ranchi(for B.Ed. course) & affiliated to J.A.C., Ranchi (for D.El.Ed. course) was established in the year 2008 for the training and Education of the upcoming generation as teachers with the prime aim to impart a highly effective and quality education to create a new breed of professionals which will make it possible for them to experience the challenges of the evolving globalization process full of opportunities for a bright career.
This college has made long strides in every sphere and has carved a special niche for itself in the field of education along with active engagement in a variety of activities such as free education, women upliftment and environment threatening issues. This college is committed to provide a forum to the students and faculty, not only to express their experiences and thoughts but also unfold their hidden talents. The college has well established Laboratories like Science Lab, Social Science Lab, Psychology Lab, Math Lab, Computer Lab, Games & Sports etc. that are needed to create an outstanding College. Currently, Santosh College of Teachers Training & education, Ranchi is running with an annual intake of 100 students in each session separately for B.Ed. & D.El.Ed. courses.
